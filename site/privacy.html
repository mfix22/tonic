[Draft] Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 12/18/2018

Use

Ranger has no intention of using your stored data in any way outside of using it to accurately invoke jobs on your repositories.

Data Storage

Ranger uses third party vendors and hosting partners to store data. Although Ranger owns the code, databases, and all rights to the application, you retain all rights to your data.

In order for Ranger to function, it must store some information about your repositories. Here is a list of the information stored:

  • Repository name
  • Repository owner's username

Questions

Please contact ranger@dawnlabs.io if you have any questions.
